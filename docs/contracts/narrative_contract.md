# Narrative Contract

## Purpose

Keep story, NPCs, quests, dialogue, locations, and daily activities aligned with
the same world logic.

## Required Fields To Resolve

- Setting
- Timeline
- Major factions
- Main conflict
- Character voice rules
- NPC relationship rules
- Quest tone
- Everyday life assumptions
- Content boundaries

## Workstream Rule

Storyline and NPC outputs must label all unresolved lore assumptions and avoid
turning them into accepted canon without Orchestrator review.
