# Shared Risk Model

Risks keep daily activities from becoming frictionless repeated clicks.

Possible risk categories:

- Tool breakage
- Resource waste
- Fatigue or stress
- Injury or safety incident
- Weather or environment penalty
- Product quality loss
- Time overrun
- Noise or attention cost
- Missed opportunity

Risks should be visible, explainable, and reducible through proficiency or better
preparation.
